Features


Restart:
The restart button will simply restart the case for you. This will reassign you to be male or female randomly and begin the case over.

Random:
The random feature can be accessed at any time by selecting the final decision out of any set of decisions. Selecting this decision will randomly make one of the other decisions in the set for you.

Angel/Devil*:
Most people should be familiar with the concept of a shoulder angel and shoulder devil. This
idea is incorporated in this feature. The feature can be activated simply by clicking on the
'Angel/Devil' button. The feature can be deactivated in the same way. While this feature
is in use the angel and devil will provide you with advice about a choice when you mouse over
a decision.

Ethical Computer*:
The ethical computer will take a given decision, apply a set of rules, and then state whether the choice is appropriate or not. The rules utilized by this ethical computer can be found in the Software Engineering Code of Ethics and Professional Practice. While the ethical computer
will not consider any other factor than its supplied set of rules, the output may help you in
making your own ethical decision. The ethical computer can be activated and deactivated by simply clicking on the 'Ethical Computer' button.

Ethical Helper:
The ethical helper will attempt to provide you with some points that you may not have considered in making your decision. Once activated the ethical helper will respond every time a decision is made. A pop-up window will appear and inform you of the decision you have made and suggest some points to consider. If after reading the suggestions you wish to make a new decision click on the rethink link and the pop-up will close otherwise click the continue link and the pop-up will close and the simulator will continue on based on the decision you made before the ethical helper appeared. Activate and deactivate the Ethical Helper by clicking on the 'Helper' button.

Code Of Ethics:
Clicking on the ‘Code Of Ethics’ button will open the code of ethics used by the ethical computer, the Software Engineering Code of Ethics and Professional Practice.

Close Window
