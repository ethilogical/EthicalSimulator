The ethical computer will take a given decision, apply a set of rules, and then state whether the choice is appropriate or not. The rules utilized by this ethical computer can be found in the (code of ethics). While the ethical computer will not consider any other factor than its supplied set of rules, the output may help you in making your own ethical decision. The ethical computer can be activated and deactivated by simply clicking on the 'Ethical Computer' button.

This concept was borrowed from Terry Winograd.
