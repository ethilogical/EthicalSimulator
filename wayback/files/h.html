The ethical helper will attempt to provide you
with some points that you may not have
considered in making your decision. Once activated the ethical helper will respond every time a decision is made. A pop-up window will appear and inform you of the decision you have made and suggest some points to consider. If after reading the suggestions you wish to make a new decision click ‘Rethink’ and the pop-up will close otherwise click ‘Continue’ and the pop-up will close and the simulator will continue on based on the decision you made before the ethical helper appeared.
